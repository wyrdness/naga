- Vatsyayan, K. (1995). *The Square and the Circle of the Indian Arts*. Abhinav Publications.

### Hindu & Buddhist Literature
- *Mahabharata* (circa 400 BCE). Epic containing major naga stories including Vasuki and the churning of the ocean.
- *Ramayana* (circa 400 BCE). Epic with naga references.
- *Buddhist Jataka Tales*. Stories featuring nagas including Mucalinda.
- *Garuda Purana*. Hindu text discussing nagas and their eternal conflict with Garuda.
